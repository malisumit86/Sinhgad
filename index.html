Sinhgad Karandak

Sinhgad College of Engineering is a technical education institute in the city of Vadgaon, Pune, India. It was established in the year 1996 with 4 UG Programs and a sanctioned intake of 240 students. The institute is affiliated with the University of Pune and managed by the Sinhgad Technical Education Society.
